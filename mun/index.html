Welcome to Fluxus Model UN 2014! We are a young MUN only in our second year of operation. A Model UN event was held for the first time in Indore, at Fluxus last year. Having received an ecstatic response from colleges as well as schools over Indore, Fluxus plans to make Fluxus Model UN a trend.

Fluxus MUN 2014 would see three Committees being simulated. Committee names and topic area would be announced shortly. The logistics would be handled by IIT Indore.
