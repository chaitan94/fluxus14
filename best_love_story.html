Best Love Story Competition

Love, its one thing that no individual living on this planet can escape from.
We are sure the cupid's arrow would have hit you hard in the bright sunshine or the dim moonlight sometime or the other!
Just like evry human being is unique, so is his love story, but unfortunately not every story survives to become famous as
the 'Romeo and Juliets!'
It may be your first crush story, the famous 'love at first sight' story or the story of your nth love.
FLUXUS 2014 brings you the chance to tell the world how far you went to express your love!
Bring out the Romeo or Juliet inside you and share your love story with us and get a chance to
win exciting prizes and a lot more surprises.
After all, pyaar kiya toh darna kya! Who knows your story might just be the best love story ever!
